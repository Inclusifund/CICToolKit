The 2-week private cold-start

Why "private"? Because the first two weeks of any onboarding are the deepest-work weeks. We protect that window. After 14 days, members opt in (or not) to public amplification via CIC Verified — separate decision, separate moment.

Onboarding bandwidth · what's included

Week 1 · Constraint mapping

10-question intake. We map your revenue model, calendar, capacity ceiling, and the 3 grant verticals you've already half-tried. No discovery call rituals.

Week 2 · Strategic allocation

We assign you 3 or 6 revenue opportunities (depending on tier). You see the full pipeline, not just the apps. Multi-year and core-cost favoured over project-based.

No FB group, no WhatsApp

Deliberate. The absence of a network signal is the signal — we're not running a funnel. Cohort communication is email + the office line.

Waitlist rollover

If May intake closes before you decide, you roll to June. No re-payment, no re-application. Just a new cold-start window.
